## Description

Tab Manager v2 is the ultimate tool for managing your browser tabs efficiently. Whether you have 10 tabs or 100, Tab Manager v2 helps you stay organized and find what you need instantly.

**Key Features:**

- **Fast Search:** Quickly find any open tab by title or URL with improved search algorithms.
- **Drag & Drop:** Reorder your tabs easily using a visual drag-and-drop interface.
- **Keyboard Shortcuts:**
  - `Control + T`: Open the popup instantly.
  - `Option + B`: Switch to your previously active tab.
  - `Control + O`: Open the extension in a new tab.
- **Dark Mode Support:** Automatically adapts to your system's light or dark appearance.
- **Clean Interface:** A modern, distraction-free UI designed for speed and usability.
- **Privacy Focused:** Your data stays on your device. We do not track your browsing history.

Tab Manager v2 is rewritten from the ground up using modern technologies to ensure the best performance and user experience.
